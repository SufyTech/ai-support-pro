# Account & Login FAQ

## I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click 'Forgot Password' on the login page. Enter your email and we will send a reset link valid for 24 hours. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

## My account is locked. What do I do?
Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts. Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock, or contact support to unlock immediately.

## How do I change my email address?
Go to Settings > Profile > Email. Enter your new email and verify it via the confirmation link we send. Your old email stops working immediately.

## How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Profile > Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data is deleted within 30 days per GDPR.

## Why was my account suspended?
Accounts are suspended for violating our terms of service, fraudulent activity, or non-payment. Contact support with your account ID to appeal.
